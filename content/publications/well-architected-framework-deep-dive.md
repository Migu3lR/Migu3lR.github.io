At AWS Summit Bogotá 2024, I presented a deep dive into the AWS Well-Architected Framework with real-world examples from enterprise customers across Latin America.

## Session overview

The session covered all six pillars of the Well-Architected Framework:

1. **Operational Excellence** — Automating runbooks and incident response
2. **Security** — Zero-trust patterns and IAM best practices
3. **Reliability** — Multi-region active-active architectures
4. **Performance Efficiency** — Right-sizing and graviton migration
5. **Cost Optimization** — FinOps practices for enterprise teams
6. **Sustainability** — Reducing carbon footprint with efficient architectures
